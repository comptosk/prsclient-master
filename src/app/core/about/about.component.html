About PRS

Welcome to my first application! It is a sample web-based Purchase Request System. It is capable of managing users, administrators, and reviewers as well as vendors, products, and requests. There is a log-in system and records are editable only by administrators or owners. Requests under $50 are automatically approved and those over $50 can be approved or rejected only by reviewers. There are still many enhancements to come. Try it yourself by heading to the login page! You can also visit my website!
